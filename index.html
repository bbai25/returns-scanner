Standard Operating Procedure

Return to Station (RTS)

DO NOT WAIT UNTIL THE END OF YOUR ROUTE!

If a delivery cannot be completed, contact Dispatch immediately.

  1. Call and text the customer.
  2. If no response, contact Driver Support.
  3. If still unable to deliver, contact Dispatch for final steps.

Failure to follow these steps will negatively impact driver scores.

Take or upload a clear package photo.

RTS submitted successfully.

Submit another package?

Before submitting, confirm Dispatch was notified.

Photo should show business hours, closure notice, storefront/signage, or changed hours.
